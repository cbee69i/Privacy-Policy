Introduction

Cbee ("we", "our", "us") operates the Cbee mobile application ("App"), which provides pet community features, rescue coordination, and location-based services.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal data. It is designed to comply with major international and regional data protection laws, including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR") and UK GDPR
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) of 2018, as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA)
  • Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) - India
  • Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (No. 26 of 2012) - Singapore
  • Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (Act 709) - Malaysia
  • Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) - Australia
  • Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) - Canada

By using the Cbee App, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

When you use our services, you're trusting us with your information. We understand this is a big responsibility, and we work hard to protect your information and put you in control. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you can manage, export, or delete it.

Information We Collect

  • Account Data:
    Collected under GDPR Article 6(1)(b) (contractual necessity).
    Includes email address, username, and encrypted password.
  • Profile Data:
    Includes profile picture, pet details, and other information voluntarily provided by the user.
    Collection based on GDPR Article 6(1)(a) (consent) and DPDP Act Section 4(2) (lawful purpose with consent).
  • Location Data:
    Collected when permission is granted. Used for navigation, rescue mapping, and nearby discovery.
    Processing based on GDPR Article 6(1)(a) (explicit consent) and CCPA §1798.100(b) (notice of collection).
    You may withdraw permission at any time.
  • User-Generated Content:
    Includes posts, images, and comments shared by the user.
    Users remain data controllers of their content under GDPR Recital 47.
  • Device and Diagnostic Data:
    Device model, OS version, app version, crash reports, and usage analytics.
    Processed under GDPR Article 6(1)(f) (legitimate interest) and CCPA §1798.140(o) (technical information).

Purpose of Processing

Processing serves the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage user accounts (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b))
  • To enable app features including location-based rescue services
  • To improve app stability and security (GDPR Recital 49)
  • To prevent fraud and misuse (DPDP Act Section 8(1))
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements (GDPR Art. 6(1)(c), CCPA §1798.100)

We do not use personal data for automated decision-making as defined in GDPR Article 22(1).

Legal Bases for Processing

Legal Basis Applicable Law Description
Consent GDPR Art. 6(1)(a), DPDP §5(1) You have explicitly consented to processing for specific purposes.
Contractual necessity GDPR Art. 6(1)(b) Processing is required to deliver app functionality.
Legitimate interest GDPR Art. 6(1)(f), PDPA §18(2) Processing ensures platform safety, security, and improvement.
Legal obligation GDPR Art. 6(1)(c), DPDP §7(1) Processing required to comply with applicable laws.

Age Restrictions

Cbee is not intended for users under 13 years of age (or under 16 years where local law requires parental consent).

We comply with the following regulations:

  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) §1302 - §1303 (U.S.)
  • GDPR Recital 38 & Article 8(1) (EU minors)
  • DPDP Act Section 9 (verifiable parental consent)

We do not knowingly collect or process data from minors.

Accessibility and Permissions Compliance

Cbee does not use Android's AccessibilityService API to perform automated actions, alter settings, or bypass user consent.

We comply with the Google Play Developer Policy (Accessibility API, 2025 revision) and ensure that all permissions are transparently declared.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share limited personal data with third-party service providers for hosting, analytics, or mapping functionalities. These providers act as data processors under GDPR Article 28(3) and are bound by confidentiality obligations.

Typical processors include:

  • Google Firebase (U.S.) - hosting, crash reporting, analytics
  • Google Maps API (U.S.) - navigation and geolocation services

We do not sell personal information (CCPA §1798.120(a)).

Data may be disclosed to law enforcement only under lawful request (DPDP Act §8(2)(b), GDPR Article 23).

International Transfers

Data may be transferred to servers outside your country.

Transfers are governed by:

  • GDPR Chapter V (Articles 44-49) - Standard Contractual Clauses
  • DPDP Act Section 16 - transfer only to compliant jurisdictions
  • PIPEDA Principle 4.1.3 - accountability for transferred data

All cross-border transfers are encrypted and monitored.

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary for its purpose or as required by law.

Data Type Retention Legal Reference
Account data Until deletion GDPR Art. 5(1)(e), DPDP §8(7)
Logs / backups 30 days Legitimate interest (security)
Analytics data Indefinite (anonymized) GDPR Recital 26 (non-personal data)

Users can request deletion under GDPR Article 17, CCPA §1798.105, or DPDP Section 12.

Security Measures

We maintain appropriate technical and organizational safeguards in compliance with:

  • GDPR Article 32 - Security of processing
  • DPDP Section 8(5) - Reasonable security safeguards
  • CCPA §1798.150(a) - Duty to protect data

Measures include:

  • HTTPS encryption in transit
  • Password hashing and salting
  • Access control and audit logs
  • Periodic vulnerability assessments

Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach:

  • We will notify relevant supervisory authorities within 72 hours in accordance with GDPR Article 33(1).
  • Users will be informed without undue delay if the breach presents a high risk to their rights, as required under GDPR Article 34.
  • Notifications will also comply with DPDP Section 8(6) and CCPA §1798.82.

Automated Decision-Making

Cbee does not engage in profiling or automated decision-making that produces legal effects on individuals, as defined under GDPR Article 22(1) or DPDP Section 9(3).

Cookies and Local Storage

Cbee does not use third-party cookies for advertising.

Any cookies or local storage used are strictly for:

  • Authentication sessions
  • Preferences
  • Performance metrics

Such use aligns with ePrivacy Directive (2002/58/EC) Article 5(3) and DPDP Section 5(2) (consent for processing).
